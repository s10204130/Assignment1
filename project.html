App Logo Design (Year 1, Semester 1)

This assignment required me to redesign an app. I chose the PayLah! app to resign. Taking into account that it is a financial app, I decided to keep the design simple. I added the hand which embodies the "paylah" action and a speech bubble to make the design more lively. Lastly I added the DBS Logo to keep the corporate branding of the app.


Designing Infographic (Year 1, Semester 1)


This assignment required me to design an infographic based on the app in the previous assignment - PayLah!. In my final design, I chose to bring back elements form my previous design such as the hand. I drew icons at the side of each paragraph to give readers a gist of what the paragraph is about. I also added the DBS Sparks to maintain their corporate identity as well as used the slogan form a PayLah adveristment for the title of the infograhic. Lastly, I addee the aeroplane which is a symbol of transfering money.


Designing A Prototype for Cardboard Game Assets (Year 1, Semester 2)


This assignment required us to come up with a design for a board game with the theme, Bermuda Triangle. This is what I came up with for the board design. I wanted to embody the mysterious factor as well as add some "adventure" elements, thus I used a map concept as the background of the board. The triangle is the playing area where players lay their chips. I decided to make a whirlpool inside the triangle to embody one of the possible reasons behind the disapearances of ships - whirlpools.

The board game my team and i created consisted of 4 characters, The Entity, Gambit, Thief and Lover. Thus, i designed the different character cards for each character, trying to embody their character through each graphic
